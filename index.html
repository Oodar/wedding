Travel & Accomodation

You will need to travel to the Ceremony and then on to the Reception. There should be enough space available in cars for people without their own transport to hitch rides between the two venues.

Parking @ Ceremony:

There are a couple of large multi-storey car parks near The Guildhall:

  • St. John's
  • Grosvenor Shopping Centre

Parking @ Reception:

If you're planning on driving to the Reception and wish to keep your car nearby, there is very limited space (2-3 spots) at the venue itself. The entrance for this is located on West Street. There is also a lot of high-street parking and if all else fails, you can usually find a quiet road somewhere to park up.

Accomodation:

There are some hotels situated in Olney itself, although availability will be limited. You can stay nearby in Milton Keyes or Northampton as Olney is only a 20-ish minute car journey from either.

  • Ramada - Milton Keynes
  • Premier Inn - Northampton
Click here for a Booking.com search based around Olney for the 4th-5th of May.

Food & Drink

The venue is dry hire, so we will be providing various alcoholic drinks. There will be prosecco, red and white wine on the tables and a beer bar with a selection of beers from Atom Brewery. You are welcome to bring any other drinks you like.

Dessert:

There will be a dessert table with wedding cake, brownies and other goodies. However, if you would like to add to the dessert table you are more than welcome to bring something tasty to share. Don't worry it's not GBBO, nobody will be judging.
